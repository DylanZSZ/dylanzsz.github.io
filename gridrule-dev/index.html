    03 · Honest boundary

    What is faithful, and what is simplified?

    Faithful transition logic

    The decision is real.

    The 7×15 topology, automatic permanent pickups, portal jumps, bad-portal failure, wrong-proof trap, exact inventory gate, three clue regimes, and 16-action no-clue oracle route match the implemented FrontierGrid instance.

    Collaborator simplifications

    The wrapper is for thinking.

    This browser port uses a fixed seed, visible buttons, optional semantic overlay, live BFS, and an adjustable evaluation budget. It does not invoke a model or reproduce the benchmark runner.

    Backlog directions

    The cards are proposals.

    Costed evidence, helpful poison, cross-case experiment ledgers, untrusted certificates, and shared intervention budgets are prompts for matched experiments. They are deliberately separate from the playable state.
